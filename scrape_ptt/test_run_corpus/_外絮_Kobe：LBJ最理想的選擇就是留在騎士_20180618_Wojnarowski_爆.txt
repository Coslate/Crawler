NBA Free Agency News: Kobe Bryant Calls Cavaliers Ideal Fit For LeBron James


One question many have had is what team would be the best fit for LeBron and L
akers legend Kobe Bryant had an interesting answer. In an interview with Benja
min Hoffman of the New York Times, Bryant explained why he believes the Cavali
ers are the ideal fit for James, while acknowledging that he could fit anywher
e:

Kobe在採訪中被問到哪一支球隊最適合LeBron James，Kobe說他相信LBJ留在騎士是最理
想的選擇。

“Yeah, Cleveland. LeBron could go anywhere and play. If he wants to stay, tha
t’s a great fit for him. Players can adapt.

是的，克利夫蘭。當然LeBron也可以在任何地方打球。但如果他能夠留下來，那對他來說
是非常合適的。球員們的適應能力都非常強。

There was a lot of talk about if Durant going to Golden State was a good fit a
nd now that seems silly to say that.

當年Kevin Durant加盟金州勇士，就有很多人討論他去那裡是否合適。如今看來，這樣的
討論非常愚蠢。

Chris Paul going to Houston was ‘is it a great fit?’ and now it seems silly
to say that.

Chris Paul加盟休斯頓火箭，人們也覺得他能夠適應嗎？現在看來，這樣的懷疑也同樣愚
蠢。

He’ll make a home wherever he decides to end up.”

無論LeBron最終去哪裡，他都會把那裡當成家一樣的。
